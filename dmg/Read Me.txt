clip and cue — Read Me
==================================================

Thanks for downloading clip and cue — a tiny menu bar app that
remembers your last 9 clipboard copies so you can paste any of
them back.


INSTALL
--------------------------------------------------
Drag the clip and cue icon onto the Applications folder
(follow the arrow above), then open it from Applications.

This build is signed with a Developer ID and notarized by
Apple, so it opens normally — no security warning.


PERMISSIONS YOU MAY BE ASKED FOR
--------------------------------------------------
  • Accessibility — so it can paste into other apps (one-time prompt).
  • Notes — only if you use "New note" from the menu.


USING IT
--------------------------------------------------
  • Click the menu bar icon to see your recent copies; click one to paste.
  • Or press  ⌘⌥V  and tap  1–9.
